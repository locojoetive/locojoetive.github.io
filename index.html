Here is a list of games I made or helped to make!

Dodge or Die

(08/2021)

@Dodge Or Die is a dodge ball game, where players take control over the ball to hit their opponent.


It was developed as part of the @Mini-Jam 86: Sports, where the limitation was No Stopping.
To meet the limitation, we designed the ball to become faster while the match progresses.


My part:
 - player & ball controls
 - arena behaviour
 - game logic
 - enemy AI
 - UI interaction
 - some juice (e.g., screen shake, tweening, etc.)

Where are My Sexy Red Boots?

(04/2021)

In @Where Are My Sexy Red Boots, your sexy red boots get stolen by the jelly Magic Man. Now it is your turn to get them back.


This game was developed for @Ludum Dare 48.
The theme and limitation were the phrase Deeper and Deeper.
We fulfilled it by making the mechanic reveal more and more details of the environment.


My part:
 - player controls
 - camera movement
 - shrinking mechanic
 - shrinking effect on the environment
 - game logic
 - appearances and behaviours of game objects
 - interactions with game objects and UI

Dungeon Soccer

(11/2020)

@Dungeon Soccer is a local-2P soccer game played in a dungeon with procedurally generated objects. The game was created as part of a little game jam organized by @Codevember.


There was no specific theme, but we formed a team of 4 people and pretty fast agreed on making a dungeon sports game.


My part:
 - player controls
 - camera movement
 - environment interaction
 - animations implementation
 - music and SFX integration
 - support in integration for procedural algorithm
 - UI interaction

Paper Fighter

(05/2020)

@Paper Fighter is a 2D platformer inspired by Kirby's Dreamland 2.


I developed it as part of my bachelor thesis about Model-Driven Game Development.
Afterwards, I extended it to have touch controls and optimized it to make it playable via mobile browsers like Chrome and Firefox.


My part:
 - everything except the title image, including
 - keyboard and touch controls
 - simple art and animations with paint
 - simple enemy AI
 - optimization for WebGL build
 - etc.

Wait A Minute Mr Postman

(08/2018)

@Wait A Minute Mr Postman is an inverse 2D platformer, created as part of the @GMTK 2018.


The theme for this jam was GENRE, but you can't MECHANIC.
We met the theme by making a 2D platformer where you control the platforms instead of the player.


My part:  - platform controls  - camera movement  - character behaviour  - implement animation  - etc.
